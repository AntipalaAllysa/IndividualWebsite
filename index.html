Out of all the songs BTS had released, this one is my favorite song so far. 고엽. This can
be translated into Dead Leaves or Autumn Leaves. One might think that it means the same thing
however commonly used term for autumn leaves is 낙엽 (nak-yeop), which makes you imagine
colourful autumn leaves while the actual title, 고엽 (Dead Leaves), means dry dead leaves that
signals the start of winter. The song talks about lost love and time, the dead leaves are metaphors of
the things they have lost and forgotten.

My First Time

James Reid and Nadine Lustre, also known as JaDine, is one of the most famous love teams here in the Philippines. Robert James Reid is the seventh child of an Aussie and a Filipina while Nadine is the eldest out of 4 siblings. It was the very first time in my life that I was interested about Filipino artists. I really didn’t take notice of any Filipino actors and actresses because I am not a big fan of Philippine movies or dramas but when I saw their movie, Talk Back and You’re Dead, I was immediately attracted to them. JaDine’s chemistry on and off the camera was just adorable that I can’t help but fall for these two. They were not good actors at first but I saw that they have room for improvement.

JaDine gave me many first times. First, I actually bought magazines to show my support( I think I have 15). Even if I don’t have money, I still buy magazines because I just can’t accept the fact that I can’t have a copy of those. For the first time, I bought an album. I really had to fight my way through many fans just to get a signed copy of James’ album. Also for the first time, I waited under the rain just to hear them sing and ran around Ayala like a crazy person in order to see a glimpse of them. I even gave up sleep just to be updated of there whereabouts. And I can still remember the night that I cried because my mother would not allow me to go to their concert. Yes, I was a die-hard fan. Every morning and night, I immediately checked JaDine’s social accounts, hoping that they post something. I still remember the moment I saw James saying “I love you”, I really cried.

I don’t know why I liked them, maybe it’s due to the fact that I have the same birthday with James Reid or that Nadine is a very kind and lovable person, but they gave me inspiration to give my best in the things that I do and be content of what I have. Even though these days I am not a die-hard fan as I was before, I will never forget JaDine and the moments that they made me happy.

Bangtan Sonyeondan

“We got different stars and stories
We got different nights and mornings.”
-Reflection by Rap Monster

Bangtan Sonyeondan, a.k.a the seven boys who ruined my life (in a good way), show their love to their fans through very simple yet admirable ways. Always being active in their Twitter account, sharing to the fans their thoughts and favorite moments, constantly doing a live stream though V live and creating music that connects them and the fans around the world.

As a way to show gratitude, ARMYs around the world show their love and care for the boys every time they visit. Through these simple projects, the fans says all the things that they cannot say to BTS. Here are some of the most unforgettable fan projects:

BTS Live Trilogy Episode III: The Wings Tour (Bangkok, Thailand)


Day 1

Day 2




For the first day of the Wings tour in Thailand, ARMYs executed their fan project which was to show this line “방탄 이란 걸 믿 어” which means “We believe in Bangtan.” while BTS is performing 2!3!, a song BTS made for the fans.
In the 2nd day of the concert, the fans did another project which was to show the line “Be Ur Wings Forever” when BTS was performing the same song. This words act as a reply from this line in the song You Never Walk Alone, “I wanna fly, But I have no wings, But your hands become my wings”, stating that ARMYs will forever be with BTS as they soar the sky.



BTS Live Trilogy Episode III: The Wings Tour (Anaheim)


Rainbow Colored Ocean



BTS 3rd Muster




When BTS started performing 2!3! (Hoping For More Good Days), the K-ARMYs surprised the boys by turning the whole arena into a purple ocean and sang along with them. Even when the music stopped playing, ARMYs' voices can still be heard throughout the whole arena, proving just how passionate they are in showing how much they love Bangtan.



BTS LIVE Hwa Yang Yeon Hwa On Stage: Epilogue (Seoul, South Korea)




On the first day of the Epilogue concert, the fans made a banner to show to the boys stating, “You will be my singer forever” while singing I need U, the very first song of The Most Beautiful Moment In Life Trilogy. During the 2nd day, the fans did another project. A banner that states, “I will be your audience forever.”, explaining that ARMYs will never leave them. Because of the fact that they come from a small company, BTS fears they may lose everything in a second but ARMYs prove their loyalty by giving the boys as much love as they can.

K-pop Legends

Sistar is a South Korean girl group formed in 2010 by Starship Entertainment. The group consisted of Hyolyn, Bora, Soyou, and Dasom. According to one member, they named themselves Sistar because they will become big stars with a friendly image, just like a sister and Sistar definitely has the sisterly image, making all of their fans feel a sisterly bond with Sistar.
For many years, Sistar’s comebacks are one of the most awaited during summer because of their catch songs. However after 7 years of being a group, the 4 women decided to go to their own separate ways. Just this May, Starship Entertainment announced that Sistar will be disbanding after releasing a last song.
